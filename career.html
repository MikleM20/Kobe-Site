Kobe Bryant was a once in a generation NBA player, he was often compared to Michael Jordan who is often called the greatest of all time, as his game often mirrored Michael's. Although many people agree that Michael was greater than Kobe, many often say that Kobe is "the most skilled player", as he had a wide variation of how he could play and seemed to be able to do it all.

Some of of his Career Achivements Include:
  • 5-Time NBA Champion: 2000, 2001, 2002, 2009, 2010
  • 2-Time NBA Finals MVP: 2009, 2010
  • NBA Most Valuable Player (MVP): 2008
  • 18-Time NBA All-Star: 1998, 2000, 2001, 2002, 2003, 2004, 2005, 2006, 2007, 2008, 2009, 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016.
  • 4-Time All-Star MVP: 2002, 2007, 2009, 2011
  • 2-Time NBA Finals MVP: 2009, 2010
