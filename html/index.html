Who was Kobe?

Unless you've been under a rock for the past couple decades you probably know who Kobe was. However, if somehow you don't know who he was then here is brief explanation of one of the NBA's greatest players.

Kobe Bryant, a former professional basketball player; a 6' 6" shooting gaurd who came from Lower Merion High School and went straight into the NBA at the age of 17. Depite being draft pick 13 for the Charlotte Hornets, he was quickly traded to the Los Angeles Lakers, where he would spend the entirity of his 20 season long NBA career. There he would win 5 NBA championship titles, 2 season MVPs, and 2 finals MVPs. Being an 18x All-Star player Kobe is to be inducted into the NBA hall of fame this year. Unfortunately January 26, 2020 he would passed away due to a helicopter crash in California.
