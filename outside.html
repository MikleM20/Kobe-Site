Family

Kobe Bryant married Vanessa Laine in April 2001 They gave birth to four daughters Natalia Diamante (b. 2003), Gianna Maria-Onore (b. 2006, d. 2020), Bianka (b. 2016) and Capri (b. 2019).



Writing

Kobe has published some books since he has retired. One of them being a book called "Legacy and the Queen", which covers a story about a girl who depite being having the odds against her pursues her passion towards tennis.

Kobe hoped to inspire others to continue to work hard to achieve their goals, and s-how that all though odds can be aginst you, hard work will always follow through to help you to achieve your goals.

More Basketball

Kobe has played for the USA internation team many times, this including 2 olympic events in which Kobe and team USA won the gold medal. After his retirement Kobe also started to coach his daughter's basketball teams, along with this he started to work out with current NBA players including players such as Jayson Tatum and the 2019 MVP Giannis Antetokounmpo, both young up-and-coming players.

Fun Fact: Jayson Tatum's favourite basketball player was Kobe Bryant.
